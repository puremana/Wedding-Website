Accommodation

For those that require accommodation in Rotorua we recommend Holdens Bay Holiday Park.

Holdens Bay Holiday Park is situated in the quiet, beautiful Holdens Bay, it is only a ten minute drive from the city centre. It is the best of both worlds with a tranquil stay, close to shops and driving distance from some of Rotorua’s main attractions. Conveniently close to Lake Rotorua, Rotorua airport and some of the best biking and mountain bike tracks Rotorua has to offer, the Redwoods is right next door.

There is a bed for every budget, ranging from non-powered and powered sites to basic cabins to fully self-contained motel units. There are shared BBQs and outdoor seating areas alongside kids play park and trampoline as well as two indoor hot pools.
